About Us

At zeroGravity, we are dedicated to providing unparalleled space exploration experiences. Our team of experts is passionate about pushing the boundaries of human potential and making space travel accessible to all.

ZeroGravity is a modern, space-themed web project designed to capture the feeling of exploring the universe beyond Earth.

The idea behind ZeroGravity is inspired by the concept of weightlessness—freedom, curiosity, and infinite possibilities.

Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the website focuses on responsive design, smooth animations, and intuitive navigation.

ZeroGravity is more than just a website — it is a journey into the unknown.
