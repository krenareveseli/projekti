Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our space missions and programs.

Is space travel safe?

All missions follow strict safety standards and are supervised by certified aerospace professionals.

Do I need training before flying?

Yes, all participants complete a professional pre-flight training program.

How long does a suborbital flight last?

Suborbital flights typically last between 5 and 10 minutes.

Can anyone join orbital missions?

Orbital missions require medical clearance and advanced astronaut training.

What age is required for space tourism?

Participants must be at least 18 years old and meet health requirements.

Is zero gravity dangerous?

Zero gravity is safe and carefully managed by our trained crew.

What should I bring to space?

All essential equipment is provided. Personal items are limited.

Are education programs available online?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online education programs.

How do astronauts sleep in space?

Astronauts sleep in specially designed sleeping bags attached to spacecraft walls.

How can I book a mission?

You can book a mission directly through our Services page.
